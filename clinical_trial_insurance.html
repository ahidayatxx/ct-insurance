Overview

Essential Protection Framework
Clinical trial insurance provides comprehensive coverage for participants, sponsors, and investigators against potential risks and liabilities during clinical research activities in Indonesia.

Key Coverage Areas

Participant Safety
Medical treatment costs, compensation for adverse events, and protection against trial-related injuries or complications.
Sponsor Liability
Product liability coverage, professional indemnity, and protection against claims arising from trial conduct.
Investigator Protection
Professional liability coverage for principal investigators and research staff conducting the trial.
Regulatory Compliance
Ensures adherence to Indonesian regulations and Good Clinical Practice (GCP) standards.
